Tabular Editor 3.21.0

  • Enhanced Direct Lake support — Build Direct Lake models faster with easier setup for Fabric Lakehouses/Warehouses and new OneLake connector support.

  • Full modeling in Desktop Edition — Unlock all semantic modeling capabilities when connected to Power BI Desktop, including modifying M expressions and managing partitions.
