- @semantic-bridge-add-object - 向指标视图添加新对象
- @semantic-bridge-remove-object - 从指标视图移除对象
- @semantic-bridge-rename-objects - 使用复制-修改模式重命名对象
- @semantic-bridge-serialize - 将指标视图序列化回 YAML
